## Description

BountyBay is a demand-first marketplace. Instead of browsing endless listings, you post a bounty for exactly what you want and let our community of verified hunters find it for you.


How It Works


Post a Bounty: Describe the item you are looking for, set a reward amount, and your bounty goes live.
Hunters Search: Our community of identity verified hunters will search for your item across the internet, local shops, estate sales, and more.
Review and Pay: When a hunter finds your item, review their submission. You only pay the reward when you accept.

What People Use BountyBay:


Discontinued products that are no longer sold in stores. Rare collectibles like vintage toys, trading cards, vinyl records, and sneakers. Sentimental items that were lost, damaged, or given away. Hard to find parts, accessories, or replacements. Regional or international products not available locally.

Built for Trust

Every hunter is verified through Stripe Identity before they can claim a bounty. Payments are held securely and only released when you accept a submission. Our support team is available to help resolve any issues.


Why BountyBay?

Traditional marketplaces make you search through millions of listings hoping to find what you need. BountyBay flips that model. You tell us what you want, and motivated hunters compete to find it for you. No browsing. No guessing. Just results.

Download BountyBay today and let someone else do the searching.
