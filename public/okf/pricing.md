# Pricing and Fees

pleasefindmethis.com uses poster-paid fees. The finder payout is the offer amount shown on the request, and pleasefindmethis.com does not take a cut from that posted finder payout.

## Formula

Poster total = finder payout + platform service fee + payment handling and source review fee.

## Fee Model

- Minimum finder payout: US$10
- Platform service fee: max(US$6, round(finder payout x 12%))
- Payment handling and source review fee: max(US$1, round(finder payout x 3%))
- Minimum poster checkout total: US$17
