# pleasefindmethis.com Overview

pleasefindmethis.com is the internet bounty board for hard-to-find items. Posters fund a finder payout for a specific item, and finders submit protected source links, seller contacts, local leads, source clues, or direct handoff options.

The platform is for exact-item searches where normal search, image search, marketplace alerts, Reddit, Discord, or asking friends has not produced a reliable source.

## What The Platform Does

- Hosts public item requests with reference images and must-have criteria.
- Lets posters fund a finder payout before the request goes live.
- Lets finders submit protected sources before reveal.
- Records reveal, review, duplicate, dispute, payout, and refund decisions.

## What The Platform Does Not Do

pleasefindmethis.com does not sell, ship, resell, authenticate, guarantee, or broker the requested physical item. If a source leads to an item purchase, the poster buys that item separately from the third-party source or seller.
