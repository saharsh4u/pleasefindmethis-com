# Marketplace Rules

pleasefindmethis.com is for lawful, safe, good-faith item sourcing. It is not for prohibited goods, unsafe transactions, harassment, privacy invasion, or bypassing rules elsewhere.

## Prohibited Requests

Do not request illegal goods, weapons, regulated or age-restricted goods, alcohol, tobacco, vapes, prescription medicines, stolen items, counterfeit documents, invasive surveillance tools, personal data, financial products, gift cards, tickets, crypto assets, NFTs, gambling-related items, pirated media, unauthorized software licenses, or items that violate a payment processor or marketplace policy.

## Source Quality

Finders should submit current, good-faith sources with enough detail for the poster to evaluate the match. Duplicate, fake, expired, bait-and-switch, intentionally vague, or unsafe sources can be rejected or marked invalid.

## Review Standards

Reveal logs, proof files, notes, timestamps, and poster decisions form the review record. A poster who reveals and uses a valid source cannot avoid the finder payout by later claiming the source was never used.
