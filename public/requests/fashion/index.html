Short answer

Find exact clothing, shoes, bags, and accessories requests work best when normal search misses the exact item and a human source may know the right listing, seller, shop, donor unit, or collector lead. Include garment type and silhouette, brand or label clues, size and fit range; verify leads with label photo and measurements.
