# Finder and Partner Outreach Plan

## Finder Recruiting Segments

Build the first batch as 80 prospects:

- 15 resellers
- 10 estate-sale or liquidation people
- 10 camera collectors or repair people
- 10 watch collectors or dealers
- 10 retro gaming repair people
- 10 electronics repair techs
- 10 local pickup helpers
- 5 Japan/proxy shoppers

## Where To Find Them

| Segment | Sources | Search Phrases | Pitch |
|---|---|---|---|
| Resellers | r/Flipping, Whatnot, eBay/Mercari bios, local reseller groups | `estate sale reseller`, `flea market finds`, `whatnot vintage electronics` | Get paid on leads you pass on |
| Estate-sale people | EstateSales.NET, HiBid, local liquidation companies | `estate sale company [city]`, `auctioneer collectibles [city]` | Extra revenue from niche leftovers |
| Camera people | Fred Miranda, Photrio, repair shops, Instagram film tags | `film camera repair`, `Leica lot`, `camera estate sale` | Paid bounties for rare gear and lots |
| Watch people | WatchCrunch, forums, local dealers | `Seiko lot`, `vintage watch dealer`, `watch repair [city]` | Bounties for references and parts |
| Retro repair | r/consolerepair, r/GameSale, repair Discords | `console repair`, `GameCube repair`, `broken console lot` | Monetize part and donor-unit knowledge |
| Electronics repair | Local shops, ham radio clubs, vintage audio forums | `vintage audio repair`, `electronics repair [city]` | You see dead stock first |
| Pickup helpers | Taskrabbit, Roadie, Craigslist gigs, FB local gigs | `pickup helper`, `errand runner`, `delivery driver with truck` | Paid scouting plus pickup bonuses |
| Japan/proxy shoppers | Buyee/ZenMarket communities, collector Discords | `Japan proxy shopper`, `Mercari Japan finds`, `Yahoo Auctions Japan haul` | Paid requests for Japan-only sourcing |

## Finder DM

```text
Hey {{name}} - saw you are active around {{category/source}}.

Quick ask: I am putting together a small paid finder network for people who already come across {{item type}}. If you spot something useful, you send photos/details. If the requester accepts it, you get paid. No listing, shipping, or customer handling unless you want pickup work too.

Would you be open to being one of the early finders?
```

## Finder Email

```text
Subject: paid finder network

Hi {{name}},

Noticed you handle {{estate sales / repairs / camera gear / watches / retro games}}.

We are recruiting a small group of early finders who can flag hard-to-source inventory before it disappears. The workflow is simple: send photos, price, location, and seller context. If a requester accepts the source, you get the bounty.

Useful for items you do not want to list yourself, leftovers after sales, broken/untested lots, or local deals you cannot personally use.

Worth trying with 1-2 finds this week?
```

## Finder Follow-Up

```text
Subject: quick one

Would it be useful if I sent a short bounty list for the kinds of items we are paying for right now?

No commitment - just send anything that matches, and we will tell you quickly if it is a fit.
```

## Finder Incentive Plan

- Base bounty: posted finder reward.
- Optional accepted qualified lead bonus during launch: $10-$25 where economics allow.
- Quality bonus: +$50 after 5 accepted leads with low cancellation rate.
- Tiers: Bronze, Silver after 5 successful accepted leads, Gold after 15 accepted leads or exclusive first-look access.
- Referral bonus: $25 when a referred finder submits their first accepted lead; $100 after their third accepted lead.
- Disqualify counterfeit, stolen, misrepresented, unsafe, prohibited, or already-submitted leads.

## Partner Offer

Launch offer:

- Partner earns 50% of the platform service fee on each first funded request they refer for the first 90 days.
- After 90 days, default to 25% of the platform service fee for referred funded requests.
- Do not share the trust/payment protection fee.
- Do not reduce the finder payout.
- Pay monthly, net of refunds, disputes, chargebacks, fraud, and canceled requests.
- Shops can also act as finders and earn the full posted finder payout separately if they submit an accepted source.
