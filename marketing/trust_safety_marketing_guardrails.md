# Trust And Safety Marketing Guardrails

This is marketing compliance guidance, not legal advice. Have counsel review final policy and payment language.

## Core Rule

Every claim must be truthful, specific, and backed before publication. If the team cannot prove a claim operationally, do not imply it in ads, landing pages, Reddit comments, influencer copy, or product UI.

## Safe Payment Protection Language

Use:

- `Pay through pleasefindmethis.com so eligible requests can be reviewed under our payment-protection process.`
- `Eligible payments made on-platform can be reviewed under our dispute policy.`
- `Source records are saved before reveal.`
- `Off-platform payments may make a request ineligible for protection.`
- `Payments are processed by our payment provider. pleasefindmethis.com does not store full card details.` Use only if true.

Avoid unless legally and operationally true:

- `100% safe`
- `scam-proof`
- `guaranteed refund`
- `fully protected`
- `risk-free`
- `insured`
- `escrow`
- `verified seller`
- `we verify every source`
- `you will get your money back if anything goes wrong`

Better wording:

- Instead of `guaranteed refund`, use `eligible disputes may qualify under the refund policy`.
- Instead of `escrow`, use `funded request` or `on-platform payment flow`.
- Instead of `verified seller`, use `requester reviews the submitted source before acceptance`.

## Prohibited Goods Summary

Suggested public copy:

```text
pleasefindmethis.com is for lawful, everyday consumer items. Do not request, source, sell, buy, or facilitate illegal, unsafe, stolen, counterfeit, regulated, age-restricted, rights-infringing, or prohibited goods or services. We may remove requests, block payments, cancel transactions, or suspend accounts.
```

Prohibited or restricted buckets:

- Illegal goods or services
- Sanctions-restricted goods or parties
- Counterfeit, replica, stolen, pirated, or IP-infringing items
- Drugs, controlled substances, prescription items, tobacco/vape, alcohol
- Weapons, ammunition, explosives, firearm parts, restricted knives
- Hazardous, recalled, unsafe, or shipping-restricted products
- Personal data, IDs, credentials, hacking tools, malware, forged documents
- Human body parts, sexual services, trafficking-related services, nonconsensual sexual content, minor sexual content
- Animals, endangered species, wildlife products
- Gambling, lottery, securities, financial products, crypto/NFT products unless explicitly approved
- Medical devices, supplements, health products, or claims requiring regulatory review

## Referral And Influencer Disclosure

Anyone with a financial, employment, personal, family, free-product, discount, referral, affiliate, or reward relationship must disclose it clearly and close to the endorsement.

Approved templates:

- `Ad: pleasefindmethis.com paid me to try the service.`
- `I may earn a reward if you sign up through my link.`
- `pleasefindmethis.com gave me credit to test this. Here's my experience.`
- `I work with pleasefindmethis.com. Sharing this because it may help with [specific problem].`
- `Sponsored by pleasefindmethis.com.`

Rules:

- Put disclosure at the beginning of short posts.
- In video, disclose in the video and caption.
- Referral pages must state reward amount, eligibility, limits, and that rewards are not tied to positive reviews.
- Influencers may only use approved claim language.
- Do not bury disclosure in profile bios, footers, "more" links, or hashtag blocks.

## Off-Platform Risk

For pleasefindmethis.com:

- Encourage users to keep request communication and platform-related payments on-platform.
- State that off-platform payments, private payment links, or fee bypass attempts may make requests ineligible for protection.

For third-party marketplaces:

- Do not market pleasefindmethis.com as a way to bypass eBay, Mercari, Poshmark, Facebook Marketplace, or seller fees.
- If a finder locates an item on another marketplace, the user should complete the purchase through that marketplace's permitted checkout/contact process.
- Do not encourage users to ask third-party sellers to move transactions off-platform.

Avoid:

- `Skip marketplace fees.`
- `Message sellers directly to get a better deal.`
- `We help you buy outside eBay/Mercari.`

## Reviews And Testimonials

Rules:

- No fake reviews.
- No AI-generated "customer" reviews.
- No employee/family reviews without disclosure.
- No testimonials from people who did not use the service.
- Do not pay for positive reviews.
- Do not imply rewards require positive sentiment.
- Do not ask only happy users for public reviews while suppressing unhappy users.
- Do not suppress negative reviews unless applying neutral moderation rules.

Safe disclosure:

```text
Reviews reflect individual experiences. Some reviewers may have received a credit or reward for honest feedback, not for a positive review.
```

Moderation rules may remove:

- Spam
- Hate/threats
- Privacy exposure
- Illegal content
- Wrong business
- Irrelevant content
- Prohibited goods
- Fraud attempts

## Reddit And Community Rules

- Read subreddit rules before posting.
- Ask mods before sharing product/referral links.
- Participate as a real member first.
- Keep promotional activity rare where allowed; stricter community rules override any general 90/10 norm.
- Always disclose affiliation when mentioning the platform.
- No astroturfing, fake customer posts, coordinated upvotes, employee comment brigades, or seeded "organic" questions.
- No unsolicited DMs to people asking for help.
- No prohibited transaction facilitation.
- If a community bans commercial mentions, help without mentioning the product.

Safe Reddit comment:

```text
Disclosure: I work on pleasefindmethis.com. If this kind of request is allowed here, one option is to post the item details and let people submit leads. If links are not allowed, ignore this and I can delete. Separately, here are the search terms I would try: [terms].
```

No-platform version:

```text
Here are the search terms I would try: [terms]. Before paying, ask the seller for [proof] and verify [detail]. Avoid anyone pushing you to DMs before sharing proof publicly.
```

## Claim Review Checklist

Before publishing, ask:

- Can we prove this claim today?
- Does it imply a guarantee?
- Does it imply seller/source verification we do not perform?
- Does it mention escrow?
- Does it hide a material relationship?
- Could it encourage prohibited goods or off-platform fee bypass?
- Does it respect the destination community's rules?
